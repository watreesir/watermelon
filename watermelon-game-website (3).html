About Watermelon Game

Watermelon Game (also known as Suika Game) is a physics-based puzzle game where players drop different fruits into a container. When two identical fruits touch, they merge into a larger fruit. The ultimate goal is to create the largest fruit - the watermelon!

Originally gaining popularity in Japan, this addictive game has become a worldwide phenomenon due to its simple yet challenging gameplay. It's perfect for quick gaming sessions but hard to put down once you start!

The game offers a perfect balance of strategy, timing, and a bit of luck, making it appealing to casual gamers and puzzle enthusiasts alike.

How to Play

  1. Drop fruits into the container by clicking or tapping where you want to release them.
  2. When two identical fruits touch, they merge into the next larger fruit.
  3. The fruit progression is: Cherry → Strawberry → Grape → Orange → Persimmon → Apple → Pear → Peach → Pineapple → Melon → Watermelon.
  4. Try to create a watermelon to achieve the highest score!
  5. The game ends when fruits stack up to the top red line and no more can be added.

Tips & Tricks

  • Plan your drops strategically - try to keep smaller fruits on the sides.
  • Watch the "next fruit" indicator to plan your moves ahead of time.
  • When you have larger fruits, try to create a stable base for them to rest on.
  • Sometimes it's better to drop a fruit quickly rather than overthinking its position.
  • If your container is getting full, focus on creating matches to free up space.
  • The sides of the container can help stabilize your fruit stack - use them wisely!
